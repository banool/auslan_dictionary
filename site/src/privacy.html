Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

Overview

Auslan Dictionary ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, which is none.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data or user information of any kind. This includes but is not limited to:

• Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
• Device information or identifiers
• Usage or analytics data
• Location data
• Cookies or tracking technologies
• Crash reports or diagnostics

No Backend Data Storage

The App does not have a backend that stores user data. There are no user accounts, no login systems, and no server-side databases containing user information.

No Analytics

We do not use any analytics services, tracking pixels, advertising frameworks, or any other mechanism to monitor your use of the App.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The App may link to external websites (such as Auslan Signbank) which have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

Children's Privacy

Because we collect no data whatsoever, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can open an issue on our GitHub repository.
